For each session, follow this checklist:

BEFORE the session:
  1. Copy SESSION-LOG-TEMPLATE.md to 01-SESSION-LOGS/[TESTER_ID]-S[N]-[YYYY-MM-DD].md
  2. Fill in the header fields
  3. Take tape measurements per 01-TAPE-PROTOCOL.md (3 takes, averaged) and fill the table
  4. Measure tester height if this is their first session (wall + tape, no shoes)

DURING the session:
  5. Set Tester ID and Session number in the harness app
  6. Set Condition label accurately (be honest about the condition — do not upgrade "solo_apartment_lighting_fail" to "assisted_studio")
  7. For controlled sessions: researcher positions the phone at ~chest height, 2-3m from tester, plain wall background
  8. For solo sessions: tester props the phone on a tripod or stack of books at chest-to-shoulder height; researcher is NOT in the room
  9. Capture Scan Pair 1 (Front + Side) — wait for "Process & Save" to complete
  10. Fill in Scan Pair 1 fields in the session log from the on-screen results
  11. Wait 2-5 minutes (tester can rest, stretch) then capture Scan Pair 2
  12. Fill in Scan Pair 2 fields

OPTIONAL (run at least once per tester, recommended first controlled session):
  13. 5-Rapid-Capture Sub-Test: without tester moving, tap Capture Front Shot 5 times in rapid succession (~10 seconds), then process each. Record the 5 waist readings in the session log.

AFTER the session:
  14. Complete the Session Observations section in the session log
  15. Add the session log file to the git commit in the sik-ai monorepo:
      cd /Users/zen/Documents/code/sik-ai

  16. Export scans.jsonl from the harness app via AirDrop to your laptop after each session
